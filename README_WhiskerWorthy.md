## What is WhiskerWorthy?

WhiskerWorthy is an app that helps you find the best food for your dog based on their breed, age, and health needs. We make feeding your furry friend simple and healthy!

## How Does It Work?

1. **Tell Us About Your Dog**: Answer three quick questions about your dog's breed, age, and any special health needs (like being a puppy, senior, pregnant, or having allergies).

2. **Get Food Recommendations**: We'll suggest the best dog food brands and types that match your dog's needs.

3. **Help Dogs Everywhere**: The information you share helps us learn more about what keeps dogs healthy and happy.

## Why Do We Collect Your Data?

When you tell us about your dog, you're helping us build a big database of information. This data helps us:

- **Test Our Ideas**: We can check if certain breeds do better on certain foods
- **Find Patterns**: We might discover that dogs with allergies do best on specific types of food
- **Help Scientists**: Veterinary researchers can use this information to learn more about dog health
- **Improve Recommendations**: The more dogs we learn about, the smarter our suggestions become

**Your Privacy Matters**: We keep your dog's information safe and only use it to make better food recommendations for all dogs.
